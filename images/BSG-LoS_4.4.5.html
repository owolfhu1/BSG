  • Each name represents all versions of characters with that name, unless specified
  • Note: Neither Cavil (support) nor Cylon leaders may ever hold any titles.
  • Note: If none of the characters chosen at the start of the game are on the Deck Chief’s LoS, remove the Deck Chief Title from the game. If one of those characters is chosen as a replacement character later in the game, that character does not receive the Deck Chief Title.
  • When the Admiral is sent to the Brig, the Admiral card is passed to the next in the line of succession. If all human players are in the Brig, the highest in succession receives the title.
  • The President exclusively controls the hand of Quorum Cards and is normally the only player that is allowed to draw more.
  • The Presidency may be passed by using the action found at the Administration location.
  • The Presidency is retained if the player is sent to the Brig.
  • Players revealed as Cylons may not possess a Title. Any titles held are forfeited and given to the next player in the line of Succession.
